Who we Are

Energy Management and Renewable energy services company.



La Peonular Energy Hub Ltd is an Energy Management and Renewable energy services company.

It was established in 2016 and has a team of dedicated professionals who bring along their wealth of expertise in energy management, renewable energy, project management, electrical engineering and business management for the growth of the company. We aim to deliver quality services and projects to all clients by working with them to:

  • Develop and set appropriate energy objectives and targets
  • Continually improve energy performance
  • Implement projects to achieve set targets
  • Purchase energy efficient products and services
  • Ensure project sustainability by training their staff on interventions deployed.

The Company has expertise in Energy audit and management, Renewable energy project designand implementation, Research and feasibility studies, Trainings and Community DevelopmentProjects. LaPeonular Energy Hub serves mainly the residential, public and commercial sectorsand has a target to be one of the leading service providers in Energy Management and Energy Efficiency in West Africa.

LaPeonular Energy Hub Ltd is currently carrying out an initiative - The “Nigerian EnergyAmbassadors” project which aims to have a network of 1 million youth energy ambassadorsnationwide. Energy clubs are being established in select educational institutions in Nigeria. The Energy Clubs focus on developing relevant knowledge and skills in energy audit and management, energy efficiency and solar PV design and installation. Alumni of the project arecurrently making their mark in the energy landscape in Nigeria.
