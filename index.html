    About Us

    Energy Management and Renewable energy services company.

    We aim to deliver quality services and projects to all clients

    An Energy Management company established in 2016, The Company has expertise in

    • Energy Audit and Management
    • Renewable Energy Project design and Implementation
    • Research and Feasibility Studies
    • Trainings and Community Development Projects

    Trainings

    Solar PV

    Participants will learn the fundamentals of solar PV feasibility assessment, design and installation

    Energy Audit and Management

    Participants will learn the rudimentary aspects and technical insights of energy audits. Hands-on training will be carried out at Small and Medium Scale Enterprises or Industries

    HOMER Software

    Participants will learn how to use the HOMER Software to carry out a mini grid hybrid designs

    Electrical Safety in RE systems

    Participants are trained on international HSE standards and best practice applicable to the Renewable energy industry

    ISO 50001 Energy Management System

    Participants will learn how to design and establish an Internal Energy Management System for any type of Organisation

    Energy Efficient Buildings

    Participants will gain insight into how to design Energy Efficient buildings in compliance with the Building Energy Efficiency Code and Guidelines
